FujiPartner

⭕️ 简介

FujiPertner是一款简洁、易用、高效的富士配方管理APP，可以让您无限添加您中意的富士胶片配方。

· 种类多样的显示方式

您可以选择配方的展示方式，详细或是简洁；可以根据配方使用的胶片模拟或是适用场景进行分类展示；同时支持搜索功能。

· 全新的配方导入与分享形式

您可以将自己拍摄的富士照片中包含配方信息一键导入，也可以导出成为海报或是二维码，以便您在社交平台上进行分享。当然，您也可以导入别人分享的配方，只需要扫描专门的FujiPartner二维码即可。

· 其他

纯净无广的App，一次买断，无需订阅，后续还有新功能升级......

⭕️ 功能介绍

· 添加配方

您有三种方式可以添加新的配方：

1：点击添加按钮后，手动输入参数。

2：从富士照片中导入配方。富士照片中包含了拍摄时的配方参数，以富士自己的方式存储在照片信息中，FujiPartner可以解析并展示这些信息。如果您拍摄了得意之作想要分享，那么就可以直接导入，免去了您在相机中查看参数的操作。需要注意的是，经过美图软件或经聊天工具压缩后的照片，往往会导致这些信息的缺失，请您尽量从原始的富士照片中导入配方信息。

3：得益于FujiPartner的分享功能，您可以通过扫描FujiPartner生成的二维码来读取配方信息，支持打开摄像头直接扫描，或是将别人分享的FujiPartner二维码保存到手机相册，然后打开相册扫描。

· 分享配方

您可以将您精心调制的配方，在FujiPartner中以二维码形式生成后分享给别人，别人用FujiPartner扫描之后就可以直接读取配方。在此基础上，FujiPartner提供了简单的海报设计样式，方便您一键生成精美的分享图片。当然，海报还是包含二维码的，可供扫描。

⭕️ Brief Introduction:

FujiPartner is a simple, user-friendly, and efficient Fuji film formula management app that allows you to add unlimited Fuji film formulas of your choice. This app has the following features:

· Diverse Display Options

You can choose how the formulas are displayed, whether in detail or a simplified view. You can categorize them based on the film simulation or applicable scenes and utilize the search function.

· New Formula Import and Sharing Methods

You can import formulas from your own Fuji photos with one click, including formula information. Additionally, you can export them as posters or QR codes for easy sharing on social media platforms. Of course, you can also import formulas shared by others; simply scan the dedicated FujiPartner QR code.

· Other Features

An ad-free app with a one-time purchase, no subscription required. There will be future updates with new features and improvements.

⭕️ Feature Introduction:

· Add Recipe

There are three ways to add new recipes:

1: Manually enter parameters after clicking the add button.

2: Import recipes from Fujifilm photos. Fujifilm photos contain recipe parameters stored in Fujifilm's own way in the photo information. FujiPartner can parse and display this information. If you have taken a masterpiece and want to share it, you can directly import it, saving you the trouble of checking parameters in the camera. Please note that photos compressed by beauty apps or chat tools often result in loss of this information, so it's best to import recipe information from the original Fujifilm photos.

3: Thanks to FujiPartner's sharing feature, you can read recipe information by scanning the QR code generated by FujiPartner. It supports directly scanning by opening the camera, or saving the FujiPartner QR code shared by others to the phone's album and then scanning it.

· Share Recipe

You can generate your carefully crafted recipes in FujiPartner and share them with others in the form of QR codes. Others can directly read the recipe by scanning it with FujiPartner. In addition, FujiPartner provides simple poster design styles for you to generate beautiful sharing images with just one click. Of course, the poster still contains the QR code for scanning.
